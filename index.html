About Me

I started my working career in the Army National Guard when I was 17.I served 6 years and deployed to Kuwait for Operation Iraqi Freedom/ Operation Enduring Freedom. While on deployment I worked in a warehouse where we ordered parts for the mechanics to fix helicopters.I also was apart of the general's driver detail where I was honored to meet General Dunwoody. She became the first female four star general! After deployment I was offered a job as a contractor for the 1106th TASMG. I worked there for 7 years and gained most of my computer knowledge there. This is where I first began to learn about coding. My husband introduced me to Geekwise Academy and I realized that I was coding at work. I'm currently a student at Geekwise taking a JavaScript class. My coding skills are impoving everyday and so far I'm proficient with HTML and CSS.
